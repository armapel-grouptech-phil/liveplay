About liveplay

Welcome to liveplay, your premier destination for accessible live streaming. Our mission is to provide a seamless, user-friendly, and centralized platform where users can discover and watch a diverse range of publicly available channels for free. We believe that access to information, entertainment, and culture should be simple and direct.

liveplay acts as an aggregator, embedding streams from various providers to bring you news, sports, entertainment, and more, all in one place.
